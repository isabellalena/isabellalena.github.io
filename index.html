My current project examines self-regulatory abilities which is operationalised through the use of a fMRI neurofeedback task. Essentially, we are comparing people's ability to modulate activity within the executive control network (ECN) and the salience network (SN), to see whether mindfulness training affects ones ability to regulate ones own brain activity.
